Liveaboards of London

As the cost of living goes up and and the housing crisis continues, the thought of pissing away the majority of your hard earned wages on painfully high rent, for a less than desirable room, in a cold and damp shared house in London for the rest of your adult life is increasingly leading people to take to the rivers. Leading marine finance lender Promarine Finance has seen a 65 percent increase in the number of liveaboards in the two years since the start of the coronavirus pandemic. (Jan 2022)
